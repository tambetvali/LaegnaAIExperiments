- Flow: implicit structures, semantics not quantified or appearing "intuitively", not as a result of plan.

It's structural knowledge is conscious:
- Conscientious for an AI: dictionary of this word """Conscientious describes someone who is thorough, careful, and guided by a strong sense of moral obligation to do their work properly. It combines high levels of organization, diligence, and responsibility, ensuring tasks are completed well rather than quickly."""
- Conscious for human: this is our conscious treshold - produced text, loogic, etc.

"Conscientious" does not expect you to be especially aware, but rather you are kind of "scientific".
